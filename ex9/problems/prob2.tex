Assume $f(x) = 1$ \\
Show Convexity:
$\forall x,y \forall t \in [0,1] f(tx+(1-t)y) \le tf(x)+(1-t)f(y)$  \\

\text{Fix x,y and t abitrary.} \\
\begin{align*}
f(tx+(1-t)y) \le tf(x)+(1-t)f(y) \\
1 \le t+(1-t) \\
1 \le 1 
\end{align*}
